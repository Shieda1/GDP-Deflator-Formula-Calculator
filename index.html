What is the GDP deflator calculator?
The GDP deflator formula calculator is a tool that determines the price level as the ratio of nominal GDP to real GDP times 100.
How to calculate GDP deflator?
To calculate GDP deflator you should use the following formula:
GDP deflator = (nominal GDP / real GDP) * 100
